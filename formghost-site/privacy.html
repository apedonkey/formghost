Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Privacy-First Promise: FormGhost is designed from the ground up to protect your privacy. Your personal information stays on your computerâ€”we never upload, store, or share your data with anyone.

What Data We Collect

FormGhost collects minimal data to provide its service:

  • Local Browser Storage: Your saved client profiles (names, addresses, emails, etc.) are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's secure storage API. This data never leaves your computer.
  • AI Field Labels: When using AI-powered form filling, only the labels of form fields (like "First Name" or "Email Address") are sent to our AI service to determine field matching. Your actual personal data is never transmitted.
  • Usage Statistics: We track the number of AI fills you've used each month (stored locally) to enforce the 50 fills/month limit on the free tier.

What Data We Do NOT Collect

We explicitly do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your personal information (names, addresses, phone numbers, emails, SSN, etc.)
  • Form data you've filled out
  • Your browsing history or activity
  • Cookies or tracking identifiers
  • Any personally identifiable information (PII)

How Your Data is Stored

All your personal data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's chrome.storage.local API. This is the same secure storage mechanism Chrome uses for passwords and bookmarks. Your data:

  • Never leaves your computer
  • Is not synced to the cloud
  • Is not accessible to other websites or extensions
  • Remains under your complete control

AI Service and Data Processing

When you use AI-powered form filling, FormGhost sends only field labels to Anthropic's Claude AI service to determine which fields should be filled with which data. For example:

What we send: "First Name", "Last Name", "Email Address", "Phone Number"

What we DO NOT send: "John", "Smith", "john@example.com", "555-1234"

The AI matching happens in real-time and the field labels are not stored by Anthropic or FormGhost after the matching is complete.

Third-Party Services

FormGhost uses the following third-party service:

  • Anthropic Claude AI: Used for intelligent field matching. Only receives field labels, never your personal data. Anthropic's privacy policy is available at anthropic.com/privacy.

Data Security

We take data security seriously:

  • All API communications use HTTPS encryption
  • Your data is stored using Chrome's secure storage mechanisms
  • We follow Chrome extension security best practices
  • No passwords or authentication credentials are stored

Your Control Over Your Data

You have complete control over your data:

  • Export: Download all your saved data as a CSV file at any time
  • Delete: Remove individual profiles or all data directly from the extension
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling FormGhost permanently removes all locally stored data from your browser

No Tracking or Analytics

FormGhost does not use any analytics, tracking scripts, or telemetry. We don't know:

  • Which websites you visit
  • How often you use the extension
  • What forms you fill out
  • Any identifying information about you

The only usage metric tracked is your monthly AI fill count, which is stored locally on your device for rate limiting purposes.

Children's Privacy

FormGhost is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of FormGhost after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
