Core Feature

Privacy-First Architecture

Your data never leaves your computer. Not "we promise not to look at it" - literally impossible for us to access.

🔒

Local-Only Storage

All your profiles are stored in Chrome's secure local storage. We can't see them, access them, or share them.

🔍

Labels Only to AI

When matching fields, we send "First Name" to the AI - never "John Smith". Your actual data stays put.

🚫

Zero Tracking

No analytics. No telemetry. No cookies. We have no idea who you are or what you're filling out.
